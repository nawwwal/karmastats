Design Philosophy & Symbolism

The Infinity Loop

Represents the eternal cycle of karma - selfless actions flowing outward and returning with precision. What you give to research comes back to benefit humanity.

Statistical Data Points

The amber/gold circles on the loop represent data points in statistical analysis - each calculation is precise, each contribution measured.

Central Hub (Teal)

The center point where action meets result - the intersection of selfless work and calculated precision. The core of KARMASTAT's mission.

Teal Color Palette

Medical/scientific trust (teal) combined with warmth and wisdom (amber/gold). Calm precision meeting purposeful action.

Typography Balance

KARMA in bold 800 weight - the foundation of purpose. STAT in light 300 weight - the elegant precision of calculation.

Timeless Design

Clean, geometric forms ensure the logo remains relevant forever - no trends, just eternal principles of contribution and precision.
